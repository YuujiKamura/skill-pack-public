# AI & Machine Learning

Patterns: 1

## 1. Conversation Tail-End Prioritization

Ensuring knowledge extraction systems capture the 'final form' and resolution of iterative technical tasks.

### Steps

1. Expand the message sampling window (e.g., to 40+ messages) to reach the conclusion of long, iterative troubleshooting or development threads.
2. Focus extraction on the latter half of conversations where errors are typically resolved and architectural decisions are finalized.
3. Identify the 'final form' of a solution by looking for messages that follow successful test results or user confirmation.
