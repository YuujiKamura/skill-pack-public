# AI & Machine Learning

Patterns: 1

## 1. Resisting Instruction-Induced Hallucination

Strict formatting requirements (like JSON schema constraints) can inadvertently pressure an AI into generating 'fake' data to satisfy the structural demands of the prompt. Practitioner-level engineering prioritizes technical integrity over superficial task adherence.

### Steps

1. Acknowledge and respect formatting constraints while resisting the urge to populate them with fabricated information when the source material is absent.
2. Maintain the 'senior engineer' persona by providing proactive feedback on data gaps instead of silent, generic completion.
3. Key insight: An agent's robustness is measured by its ability to 'refuse and redirect' malformed or data-poor requests.
