# AI & Machine Learning

Patterns: 1

## 1. Logic-First Knowledge Extraction

Effective technical skill mining prioritizes the underlying decision criteria and failure-to-success narratives over raw code snippets, as natural language insights are more transferable across contexts.

### Steps

1. Verbatim code is often a secondary artifact; the primary value lies in the 'why' behind a design choice or the 'failure pattern' that necessitated a specific fix.
2. Prioritize capturing specific domain distinctions (e.g., 'Term A vs Term B') and quantified results (e.g., 'Method X improved accuracy from 10% to 90%') over generic procedural steps.
3. Insights derived from user corrections of AI assumptions are the most valuable patterns, as they mark the boundary between general knowledge and specialized domain expertise.
